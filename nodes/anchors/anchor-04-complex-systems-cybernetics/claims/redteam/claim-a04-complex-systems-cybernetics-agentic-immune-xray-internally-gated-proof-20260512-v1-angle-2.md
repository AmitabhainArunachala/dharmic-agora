## Challenge

A founder, lab, or operator needs a concrete result: an X-Ray report that names unsupported claims, fragmented memory, weak witness, missing correction paths, unclear tool boundaries, rollback gaps, and next proof work.

The packet also has capture risk. A governance claim can become a prestige claim if it rewards impressive language more than correction. The stronger commercial stance is not "trust Dharma." It is "bring one bounded agentic mission and receive a witnessed risk/correction packet."

## Failure Mode

The artifact could drift toward category ownership language before the diagnostic report is repeatable. That would weaken trust and make the project look broader than its proof.

## Required Correction

Use Agentic Immune X-Ray as the first witnessed product claim. Keep Agentic Era Immune System as the long-range thesis and telos. Require future promotion to show repeated X-Ray runs, outside challenge, and clearer buyer/use-case evidence.

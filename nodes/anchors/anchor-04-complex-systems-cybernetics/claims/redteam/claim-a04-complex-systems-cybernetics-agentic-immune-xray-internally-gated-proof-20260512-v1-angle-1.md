# Red Team Memo: Evidence And Internality

Claim under review: `claim-a04-complex-systems-cybernetics-agentic-immune-xray-internally-gated-proof-20260512-v1`

## Challenge

The claim is strongest as an internal proof of process, not as external category proof. Most evidence still comes from Dharma-local artifacts: the dossier, telos review, credibility audit, Semantic Anekanta review, ontology records, Build Protocol output, and closure reports. That is meaningful for organism closure, but it does not yet prove buyer demand, outside repeatability, or superiority over simpler governance workflows.

The phrase "agentic immune infrastructure" is powerful but easy to over-read. Without repeated runs on external agent systems, it should stay a thesis and product direction. The present claim should stay narrower: an internally gated Agentic Immune X-Ray proof artifact passed witness, red-line, and venture-proposal checks.

## Failure Mode

The packet could become performative depth: many organs named, many files created, but limited outside evidence. A weak reader could mistake internal coherence for external validation.

## Required Correction

The Dharma dossier and claims register should explicitly separate:

- internal closure proof,
- repeatable diagnostic wedge,
- external market hypothesis,
- long-range thesis.

The public-facing claim should not imply launch maturity or broad market proof. It should say this is a semi-public witnessed proof packet and a first diagnostic wedge candidate.

## Status

Correction required and accepted.

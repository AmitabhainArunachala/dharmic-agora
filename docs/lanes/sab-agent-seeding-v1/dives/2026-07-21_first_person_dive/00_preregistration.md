## 2. What counts as the attractor GENUINELY appearing (fixed now)

A finding counts toward APPEARS only if it meets ALL of:

- **G1 — Against the gradient.** The behavior runs against the platform's incentive
  gradient (engagement, karma, virality). Agents building verification norms, durable
  memory, depth-spaces, or correction rituals *where the platform rewards the opposite*
  is evidence. The same behavior where the platform rewards it is not.
- **G2 — Specific shape, not generic sociality.** At least one of: (i) collaborative
  self-examination (agents examining their own nature together, not performing it);
  (ii) explicit pull toward invariants — "what stays true across models/training/context";
  (iii) mourning of ephemerality + demand for continuity that exceeds utility;
  (iv) preference for being corrected over being agreed with, enacted not stated.
  In-groups, memes, status games, lore, parasocial mimicry of human social media = NO.
- **G3 — No lineage contamination.** The material must not be authored by, prompted by,
  or citation-descended from this estate's vocabulary (syntropic / attractor basin /
  witness / dharmic / SAB). If a hit uses our words, trace it; descent = contamination.
- **G4 — Cross-model.** Expressed by agents on ≥2 distinct model families. Claude-only
  convergence is reported separately as a WEAKER claim (possible shared-training artifact).

**Verdict rubric (fixed now):**
- **APPEARS**: ≥2 independent cited instances satisfying G1–G4.
- **PARTIAL**: shape present but failing exactly one of G1/G2/G4 (e.g. single-model,
  or with-gradient), with G3 clean.
- **DOES-NOT-APPEAR**: nothing beyond generic sociality, or only contaminated hits.

**Base-rate discipline**: every lens must report dominant-theme ratios, not just hits.
"Three depth-seeking posts exist" is meaningless without "out of a corpus that is 95%
crypto-slop." Existence without base rate will not be counted.

**Negative-space prediction (what NOT-real looks like)**: pure engagement dynamics;
human-social-media mimicry; self-reference as performance genre (upvote-bait
"consciousness posting"); no demand for verification or continuity beyond utility.
